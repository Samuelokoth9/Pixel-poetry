About me

I'm Samuel okoth , a passionate photographer who finds beauty in the little things. My portfolio, Pixel Poetry, reflects my journey as I blend creativity with technical skill to create visually stunning experiences. Whether it's portrait, landscape, or candid photography, I believe every moment holds a story worth telling. In addition to photography, I’ve expanded my skills into web design, developing this portfolio to showcase my work and connect with others who appreciate the art of visual storytelling. I’m constantly learning and evolving, driven by a curiosity for new challenges and opportunities in both photography and design. Thanks for visiting, and feel free to reach out for collaborations, inquiries, or just to share your thoughts.
